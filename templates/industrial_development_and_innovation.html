Industrial Development & Innovation

Sustainable industrialization and innovation underpin economic diversification, job creation, and resilient infrastructure—a core pillar of SDG 9. UNIDO facilitates structural transformation by building local industry capacities for value addition, export promotion, and decent jobs in industry and related services. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

Global Framework: SDG 9 & UNIDO’s Mandate

SDG 9 calls for resilient infrastructure, inclusive industrialization, and innovation. As a UN specialized agency, UNIDO aims to eradicate poverty through inclusive and sustainable industrial development, aligning economic, social, and environmental dimensions for future generations. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

The **Industrial Development Report 2024** emphasizes digitalization, job creation, and decarbonization as pivotal to modern industrial policy—a roadmap for developing and least‑developed countries alike. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

  • One District One Factory: Decentralizing industrial development across all districts. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}
  • Strategic Anchor Industries: Diversifying the economy through key sectors. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

Cluster Development & Innovation
